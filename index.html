Meet Our Riders!

A Planks ski conference is a multi-day event that brings together professionals, enthusiasts, and stakeholders from the skiing and snow sports industry. Held at or near popular ski resorts, the conference combines educational sessions, keynote speakers, panel discussions, and networking opportunities with on-mountain activities such as skiing, snowboarding, and gear demos.
